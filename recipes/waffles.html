Belgain Waffles

Description

This recipe uses common baking ingredients and an electric waffle iron to make Belgain Waffles. In English-speaking countries, Belgian waffles are a variety of waffle with a lighter batter, larger squares, and deeper pockets than American waffles.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Warm milk
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Begin preheating the waffle iron
  2. Melt the butter and put it into a large mixing bowl
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients
  4. Add all wet ingredients (aside from the eggs) to the butter bowl and mix
  5. Add the eggs to the rest of the wet ingredients and mix again
  6. Continously mix the wet ingredients while adding the dry
  7. Once fully incorporated, add an appropriate amount of batter to the pre=heated waffle iron and cook per the iron's instructions
  8. Remove cooked waffle from the iron and place on a wire cooling rack
  9. Repeat steps eight and nine until no batter remains
