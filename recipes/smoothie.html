Kale Tropical Smoothie Recipe

Kale Tropical Smoothie

Description

This Kale Pineapple Smoothie is absolutely delicious. Thick, creamy, sweet, and loaded with flavor. It's also is packed with superfoods and super healthy.

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Pineapple
  • Apple
  • Orange
  • Banana
  • Coconut Milk

Steps

  1. Wash kale, peel banana, core apple and discard core.
  2. Peel orange, separate into slices and discard seeds
  3. Chop kale into managable pieces
  4. Cut apple, pineapple, and banana into one to two inch chunks
  5. Add all ingredients to blender and blend on high until smooth
  6. Pour into glasses and enjoy
