Scotch Egg

Description

A Scotch egg is a boiled egg wrapped in sausage meat, coated in breadcrumbs and baked or deep-fried. This recipe uses the deep fried option.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Panko bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Prep a deep frier at 400 degrees
  2. In a bowl, combine ground beef, salt, pepper, cilantro, and worcestershire sauce
  3. Soft boil egg
  4. Wrap the soft boiled egg in the ground beef mixture
  5. Crust the wrapped egg in bread crumbs
  6. Deep fry for 7 minutes
  7. Remove and rest on cooling rack with tray or paper towels underneath
